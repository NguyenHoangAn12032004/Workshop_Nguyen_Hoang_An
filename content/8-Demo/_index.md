{{% notice tip %}}
**How to stream/display your own video directly?**
1. Upload your demo video to Google Drive.
2. Right-click the video -> Choose **Share** -> Set access to **"Anyone with the link"** (Viewer).
3. Open the video in Google Drive -> Click the **3 dots** icon in the top right -> Choose **Open in new window**.
4. In the new window, click the **3 dots** again -> Choose **Embed item...**.
5. Copy the source `iframe` code and update the `src` attribute (or just copy the ID token between `/d/` and `/preview` to replace it in this file).
